BIO

Chase The Who? Chase The Bear is a Canadian rock band that's all about the hooks, adding a dynamic blend of soul and pop influences to their music. The band has performed over 150 shows, including a sold-out opening slot for The Trews at Vancouver’s iconic Commodore Ballroom. Started as a busking duo and quickly evolving into a 6-piece band of likeminded people who have bonded as brothers and brought their musical chemistry and infectious energy to crowds all over Canada. Signed with Vancouver-based record label Rock Is Dead Records (604 Records/Warner Music Canada) and releasing their debut album “Honey” in 2023. 2024 promises to be Chase the Bear’s biggest year yet.


"Inspired by the likes of The Trews and The Glorious Sons, Chase The Bear, given time and resources, could join the ranks of these successful Canadian rock bands." -Darrell Gilmour (Vice President, Macklam Feldman Management)


“You must see this band. Let’s help them establish a core base and turn this into their proper new home … Chase The Bear were relentless from start to finish.” -Jon Williams (DJ and Host, What Was That Radio)
